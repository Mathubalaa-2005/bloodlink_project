When you registered as a donor or requestor and then reloaded the website, your data was lost. This happened because the data was only stored in the computer's memory (RAM), which gets erased when you restart the application.

## Where is My Data Stored?

Your data is saved in files in the `data/` folder:
- **donors.json** - All donor registrations
- **requestors.json** - All requestor registrations
- **blood_requests.json** - All blood requests
- **donations.json** - All donation records
- **inventory.json** - Blood bank inventory
- **assignments.json** - Donor-request matches

These files are created automatically on first run.
